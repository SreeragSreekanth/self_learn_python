Python Coding Questions: Decision Making

1. Even or Odd
Write a program to check whether a number is even or odd.

2. Positive, Negative, or Zero
Take a number as input and determine if it is positive, negative, or zero.

3. Voting Eligibility
Check if a person is eligible to vote (age ≥ 18).

4. Largest of Two Numbers
Write a program to find the larger of two numbers.

5. Grade Calculator
Input marks and assign grades:
90+ → A
75–89 → B
50–74 → C
Below 50 → Fail

6. Simple Calculator
Take two numbers and an operator (+, -, *, /) and perform the operation.

7. Leap Year Checker
Check whether a year is a leap year.

8. Electricity Bill
Calculate bill based on units:
First 100 units → ₹5/unit
Next 100 → ₹7/unit
Above 200 → ₹10/unit

9. Day of the Week
Use match-case to print the day name based on a number (1–7).

10. Menu-Based Program
Display a menu:
1. Add
2. Subtract
3. Multiply
4. Exit
Use match-case to perform the chosen operation.
